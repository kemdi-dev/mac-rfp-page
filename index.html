RFP Assistance

Need help picking the right Marketing Automation Platform for your business? We’re here to help! Tap us in to identify your needs, provide you with un-biased perspectives, and help you through the selection process.

COMMON OVERSIGHTS WE SEE ARE

As a Marketer, you know the value that Marketing Automation brings to a business, helping to supercharge company growth, marketing and sales. However, choosing the wrong platform for your business can be a costly mistake, setting back a company years with a fixed contract.

Tool too complex for day-to-day users at the company
Problems with existing data model syncing with marketing automation tool
Conflicts between processes within CRM and marketing automation
Marketing automation tool not supporting custom CRM objects
Marketing automation tool not syncing with specific TechStack of company
Legacy marketing at the company that’s difficult to recreate in marketing automation
Price of marketing automation platform or version of platform doesn’t make sense from ROI perspective
Navigating the pros and cons of each platform can be a daunting task. At MAC, we have worked deeply in all the major Marketing Automation platforms for a wide range of companies, both big and small, and across various industries. With such a vast array of experience under our belt, we are confident that we can help you choose the Marketing Automation platform that is best for YOU.
